Research & Diligence

Deep research designed to validate or kill a thesis early.

Our team originates themes, builds screening universes, and advances ideas through a staged research funnel. We combine quantitative analysis with first-hand conversations to sharpen conviction.

Primary Research

  • Management interviews and leadership assessments.
  • Customer calls and channel partner feedback.
  • Conversations with former employees, suppliers, and competitors.

Fieldwork & Financial Analysis

  • Site visits and operating walk-throughs when appropriate.
  • Quality of earnings and cash conversion diagnostics.
  • Sensitivity testing across downside and execution scenarios.

Risk Assessment

Key risks, mitigants, and pre-mortem analysis are assessed before LOI and again pre-close.

Operator Validation

Operators pressure-test feasibility of specific value levers and resource plans before commitment.

Decision Discipline

Opportunities advance only when controllable value drivers are clear and downside is sized conservatively.
