Case Studies

Illustrative work focused on process over performance.

The examples below highlight how we evaluate situations, structure diligence, and execute value-creation plans.

Example A: Specialty Services Platform (Anonymized)

Situation: Founder transition and succession planning.

Thesis: Fragmented niche with recurring revenue and pricing headroom.

Diligence: 20+ customer calls, SKU-level margin analysis, and route density study.

Plan: Pricing program, technician utilization, and tuck-in M&A pipeline.

Outcome: Stabilized churn, expanded EBITDA margins, and completed first add-on.

Example B: Industrial Components Carve-out (Anonymized)

Situation: Corporate divestiture with stranded costs.

Diligence: TSA mapping, working capital diagnostics, and supplier renegotiation plan.

Plan: Stand-up finance, procurement savings, and salesforce rebuild.

Outcome: Qualitative and compliance-appropriate operating improvements.

Disclosure

Case studies are anonymized and illustrative. They are provided to describe process and do not represent all investments or expected future results.
