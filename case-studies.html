Illustrative Work

Process-first case studies.

We emphasize decision quality, execution discipline, and qualitative operating outcomes rather than performance marketing.

Example A: Specialty Services Platform

Situation: Founder transition and succession planning.

Thesis: Fragmented niche with recurring revenue and pricing headroom.

Diligence: 20+ customer calls, SKU-level margin analysis, and route density study.

Plan: Pricing program, technician utilization, and tuck-in M&A pipeline.

Outcome: Stabilized churn, expanded EBITDA margins, and completed first add-on.

Example B: Industrial Components Carve-out

Situation: Corporate divestiture with stranded costs.

Diligence: TSA mapping, working-capital diagnostics, and supplier renegotiation planning.

Plan: Stand-up finance function, procurement savings, and salesforce rebuild.

Outcome: Improved conversion cycle visibility, procurement savings realization, and cleaner operational reporting cadence.
