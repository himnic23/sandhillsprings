Investment Approach

Preserve capital first. Compound it second.

We are long-term, research-driven investors. We invest where value creation can be influenced by execution and partnership, not by market noise.

Our Philosophy

We seek opportunities where we can be a constructive partner to management and where strategic and operational improvements are within our control. Discipline in selection and underwriting is central to every commitment.

Investment Criteria

Sectors Business services, specialty manufacturing, and software-enabled services.
Company Profile Typically established businesses with strong cash conversion, identifiable margin levers, and diversified customer relationships.
Check Size / Ownership $25M-$150M for control, co-control, or minority positions with governance protections.
Geography US West, broader North America, and selected markets where we can support outcomes directly.
Situations Founder transitions, corporate carve-outs, buy-and-build platforms, and select special situations.

How We Source

  • Proprietary outreach to founders and executives.
  • Operator and advisor referrals.
  • Targeted intermediaries in focus verticals.
  • Thematic research leading to company-specific outreach.

How We Underwrite

  • Unit economics and cohort analysis for revenue quality and pricing power.
  • Cash conversion review, including working capital and capex intensity.
  • Customer and market work: voice-of-customer, win/loss, and competitive mapping.
  • Operator-led assessments with 100-day planning and KPI design.
  • Downside protection through structure, covenants, and conservative base cases.
