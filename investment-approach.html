Investment Approach

Preserve capital first. Compound it second.

We are long-term, research-driven investors. We avoid reacting to market noise and focus on companies where value creation can remain within our control.

We seek to be constructive partners to management teams through disciplined underwriting and a practical post-close execution plan.

How We Source

  • Proprietary outreach to founders and executives.
  • Operator and advisor referrals.
  • Targeted intermediaries in our focus verticals.
  • Thematic research leading to company-specific outreach.

How We Underwrite

  • Unit economics: revenue quality, retention, and pricing power.
  • Cash conversion: working capital intensity and capex requirements.
  • Customer and market work: voice-of-customer and competitive mapping.
  • Operator-led assessments with 100-day planning and KPI design.
  • Downside protection via structure and conservative case planning.
