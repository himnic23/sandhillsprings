What We Do

Three pillars guide every investment decision.

We seek control or significant minority ownership in situations where patient capital, deep diligence, and operational partnership can create enduring enterprise value.

Focus

Control or significant minority stakes in business services, specialty manufacturing, and software-enabled services through founder transitions, carve-outs, and special situations across North America.

Discipline

Intrinsic value underwriting, downside protection, and multi-year holding periods designed to preserve capital first and compound second.

Partnership

Structured support in pricing, sales excellence, procurement, working capital, and digital initiatives to accelerate practical, measurable improvements.

Why It Works

We combine concentrated investing with operator-led diligence and post-close value-creation playbooks. Our team and advisors bring deep operating and investing experience across our target sectors, helping us move from thesis to execution with speed and clarity.

Where We Lean In

  • Revenue models with resilience and pricing power.
  • Markets with fragmentation and consolidation potential.
  • Management teams aligned around long-term value creation.

How We Engage

  • Collaborative strategic planning and operating cadences.
  • Clear KPI frameworks that tie action to value drivers.
  • Access to advisors, operators, and functional specialists.
